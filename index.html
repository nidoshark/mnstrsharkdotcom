The Dungeon Hates You is a Hack n' Slash platformer roguelite with elements of time and item management. Delve the many stages of the Dungeon as totally-not-pressured-into-it hero, proving your worth against a wide range of monsters and bosses!

Development on the current iteration of the game started in July 2021 (Now being ported to a more proficient engine as of 2022)

We're on track to release the first public demo by the end of the year, so please keep an eye or two (or more!) out for future updates on the project.
